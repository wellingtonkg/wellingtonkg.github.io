Frequently asked questions

Common questions from companies exploring digital transformation and custom software solutions.

How do we get started with PlugDig?

The process is straightforward:

  1. Initial Consultation (Free): 30-minute call to understand your needs, timeline, and goals
  2. Proposal: We provide a detailed proposal with scope, cost, and timeline
  3. Kick-off: Upon agreement, we assign a dedicated project manager and begin work
  4. Regular Updates: Weekly status meetings and transparent communication throughout

Schedule your free consultation →

How long does a typical project take?

Timeline depends on project scope and complexity:

  • Simple integrations: 2-4 weeks
  • Custom web applications: 3-4 months
  • Enterprise transformations: 6-12+ months
  • Ongoing support: Flexible retainer arrangements

We structure projects in phases to deliver value incrementally and adapt to your business pace. In our case studies, we've delivered complex systems in 6 months that competitors estimated at 2 years.

What companies do you typically work with?

We work with companies across multiple sizes and industries:

  • Startup to Mid-market: 50-500+ employees
  • Vertical industries: Financial services, manufacturing, logistics, retail, healthcare, government
  • Growth stage: Companies scaling operations and needing technical capability

What defines our ideal clients:

  • Clear understanding of their business need
  • Budget allocated for quality execution
  • Willingness to partner (not just hand off the work)
  • Leaders invested in the outcome
How much does custom software development cost?

Cost varies significantly based on scope, complexity, and timeline. Our typical engagement models:

  • Fixed-Price Projects: $50K - $500K+ (timeline-bound scope)
  • Time & Materials: $150-$250/hour depending on specialization and team mix
  • Retainer Model: $5K - $20K+/month for ongoing support and capacity
  • Dedicated Team: Custom pricing for full-time team augmentation

We typically see ROI within the first 6-18 months through automation, efficiency gains, and revenue impact. Contact us for a custom estimate →

What's your development process?

Our proven approach:

  1. Discovery & Diagnosis: Deep dive into your business, pain points, and objectives
  2. Architecture & Planning: Technical design, technology selection, and risk mitigation
  3. Phased Execution: Build highest-impact features first, test thoroughly, iterate based on feedback
  4. Continuous Delivery: Deploy working software frequently, not just at the end
  5. Quality Assurance: Automated testing, security reviews, performance optimization
  6. Knowledge Transfer: Your team learns and maintains the system long-term

We emphasize collaboration over hand-offs, working closely with your team throughout.

Do you handle integrations and third-party systems?

Yes. We specialize in enterprise integrations:

  • ERP Systems: SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, NetSuite
  • APIs & Data: REST/GraphQL APIs, real-time syncing, data pipelines
  • Cloud Services: AWS, Azure, GCP integrations and migrations
  • Legacy Systems: Connecting modern apps to older systems safely
  • Third-Party Platforms: Salesforce, Slack, Jira, custom vendor APIs

Our case studies include zero-downtime migrations and complex multi-system integrations across 50+ countries.

What about post-launch support?

We provide flexible support options:

  • Handoff Support (30-90 days): Included to ensure smooth transition to your team
  • Retainer Support: Monthly retainer for ongoing maintenance, updates, and optimization
  • Break-Fix Support: Pay-as-you-go hourly support when issues arise
  • Feature Development: Continue advancing the system with new capabilities

We document everything thoroughly so your team can maintain the system independently if you choose to.

How do you handle confidentiality and data security?

Security is non-negotiable:

  • NDA: We sign comprehensive NDAs before discussing sensitive information
  • GDPR & CCPA Compliance: Full compliance with data protection regulations
  • Encrypted Data: All data transmission over HTTPS; sensitive data encrypted at rest
  • Security Audits: Regular security reviews for vulnerabilities
  • Access Control: Limited access, role-based permissions, audit logging
  • ISO Standards: We maintain industry-standard security practices

See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for complete details.

What happens if the timeline slips?

We're transparent about risks:

  • Weekly Updates: You see progress in real time, no surprises at the end
  • Realistic Estimates: We build in buffer for unknowns (we've been burned by optimistic estimates too)
  • Early Warning: If we identify timeline risk, we reach out immediately with options
  • Options: Reduce scope, adjust timeline, or increase team size — your choice

In our experience, proactive communication prevents 90% of timeline problems.
